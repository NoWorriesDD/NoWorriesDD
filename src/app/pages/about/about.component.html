About Our Project

Our team embarked on a comprehensive analysis of Yelp data, delving into various facets of user behavior, merchant trends, and review dynamics to unveil insightful patterns and trends. Leveraging advanced data analysis techniques, we meticulously dissected the dataset to extract valuable insights, enabling businesses to make informed decisions.

Our team implemented sentiment analysis for reviews by leveraging techniques. We utilized big data models to analyze the sentiment of user reviews, extracting key sentiments such as positive, negative, or neutral. By processing the text of reviews, we quantified the overall sentiment of each review, enabling users to gauge the general sentiment towards a merchant. This allowed us to generate concise summaries of reviews, providing users with valuable insights into customer experiences
